Take cells from healthy part of infected organ, using needles and syringe (if possible).

See: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_human_cell_types_derived_from_the_germ_layers

sterelise & disinfect container

chop up cells in container

add culture medium (for instance EMEM, DMEM or other) solution [see https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eagle%27s_minimal_essential_medium]

heat to 37 degrees celcius

leave for 18 to 24 hours





remove cancerous tissue without the use of heat or radiation (a low oxygen room is also a bonus), freezing may help/work

affix/dump the contents of the container plate on chopped out/missing parts of the organ (pretty much like sculpting)

sew back up
